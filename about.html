About Our Barangay

Learn about our community's rich history, values, and the dedicated leaders serving you.

Our History

Established during the Spanish colonial period, our barangay has grown from a small settlement into a vibrant community. Through the years, we have preserved our cultural heritage while embracing progress and modernization.

Today, we stand as one of the most organized barangays in the city, with active community programs, transparent governance, and a vision for a better tomorrow for every Filipino family.

"Bayanihan" — the spirit of communal unity and cooperation

Our Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and accessible public services to every resident. We commit to fostering a peaceful, progressive, and inclusive community where every voice is heard and every family is cared for.

Our Vision

A model barangay that is recognized for its clean governance, active citizen participation, sustainable development, and empowered residents who contribute to nation-building.
